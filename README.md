# ARC Miner
### The First Intel Arc GPU Pearl (PRL) Miner

High-performance Pearl mining software optimized for Intel Arc B580 and B570 GPUs,
achieving **31 TH/s at 160W**

---

## Performance

| Hardware | TH/s | Power |
|----------|------|-------|
| Arc B580 | 31 TH/s | 160W |

---

## Requirements

Intel Arc B580 (12 GB) or B570 (10 GB) — tested. Other Arc GPUs with ≥10 GB VRAM may work but are untested.
Latest Intel Arc GPU drivers.
Windows 10 or 11, 64-bit.
