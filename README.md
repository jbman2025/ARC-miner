## Requirements

- Intel Arc A or B series card (8gb+)
- B580 (12gb) A750 (8gb) — tested. Other Arc GPUs with ≥8GB VRAM may work but are untested.
- Latest Intel Arc GPU drivers.
- Windows 10 or 11, 64-bit.

---

edit the A1start.bat or A1start-POOLNAME.bat with your wallet and workername.
